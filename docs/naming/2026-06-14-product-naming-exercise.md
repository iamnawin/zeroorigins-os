## What the Product Does

Three unified layers:

### 1. Internal Operating World
- Daily check-ins, team updates, internal tasks
- Projects, ideas, decisions, ownership
- Files / links / notes / status tracking / activity history

### 2. Revenue Operating World
- Leads, customers, partner requests
- Contracts, proposals, service requests
- Revenue opportunities, delivery follow-ups, relationship history
- Partner contribution / commission tracking

### 3. AI Guidance World
- AI business briefing as the first screen
- "Here is what needs attention today"
- "These leads need follow-up"
- "This project has no update"
- "Suggested action: assign this, follow up on that, convert this lead"

**Product essence:** A unified business operating workspace for small teams where internal work, revenue motion, partnerships, and AI-guided execution come together.

---

## Brand Feeling Requirements

- Unique, ownable, memorable
- Modern, slightly tech, practical
- Not childish, not fake Latin perfume
- Not coworking-space name
- Not too enterprise-heavy, not too developer-only
- Suitable for small businesses and growing teams

**Market:** Global, open to .io or .ai TLDs  
**Avoid:** Generic endings (ly, ify, io, base, stack, ops, hub, desk, board, grid, flow) unless the name is genuinely strong  
**Avoid overused words:** Ops, Core, Grid, Flow, Hub, Desk, Board, Pilot, Command, Nexus, Axis, Layer, Runtime, Signal, Pulse, Work, Task, CRM, AI, Bot

## Final Top 5 — Deep Dive

---

### #1 — READOUT `57/70`

**Homepage hero line:** Your daily business readout.

**1-line product description:** Readout is the AI-guided operating workspace that gives small teams a daily, intelligent summary of their work, revenue, and priorities — so they always know what's happening and what to do next.

**Taglines:**
1. "Every signal. Every morning."
2. "Read your business like a sensor."
3. "What's happening. What matters. What's next."

**Why this becomes a real SaaS brand:** Creates a new category — the AI business readout. Like a medical monitor gives a readout of vital signs, Readout gives a real-time readout of business vital signs. Not a dashboard. Not a CRM. Your business readout. Users will say "let me check the readout" within a week.

**Why better than ZeroOrigins OS:** ZeroOrigins OS sounds like internal developer tooling. Readout sounds like a product anyone would buy. One word, no punctuation, no "OS" jargon. Stands entirely alone.

---

### #2 — RIVET `56/70`

**Homepage hero line:** Work. Revenue. Riveted together.

**1-line product description:** Rivet is the unified workspace that permanently connects your team's internal work, revenue motion, and AI guidance — so nothing slips through and nothing goes unnoticed.

**Taglines:**
1. "Everything connected. Nothing missed."
2. "Rivet your team to what matters."
3. "The workspace that holds."

**Why this becomes a real SaaS brand:** Has the phonetic profile of the best SaaS names: two syllables, hard consonant, instant pronunciation. Same class as Linear (direction + precision), Stripe (one clean line). Double meaning — connect + captivate — creates depth without confusion.

**Why better than ZeroOrigins OS:** "I use Rivet for my team" is a sentence a real founder says in public. "I use ZeroOrigins OS" is not.

---

### #3 — VOXARA `55/70`

**Homepage hero line:** Your business, speaking clearly every morning.

**1-line product description:** Voxara is an AI-guided business workspace that narrates what's happening across your work, revenue, and team — and delivers clear, actionable intelligence so you always know what to do next.

**Taglines:**
1. "The voice your business needed."
2. "Don't read your business. Hear it."
3. "Work. Revenue. Spoken clearly."

**Why this becomes a real SaaS brand:** Trademark-clean, domain-available, zero brand interference. As AI voice interfaces grow, Voxara will feel prescient. The -ara ending is globally pronounceable across Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, and Hindi.

**Why better than ZeroOrigins OS:** Can grow into a global brand without carrying the company name as a prefix. Stands alone on any continent.

---

### #4 — MUSTER `54/70`

**Homepage hero line:** Muster your team. Know your business.

**1-line product description:** Muster is the daily operating workspace for small teams — where internal work, revenue, and AI-guided priorities come together so the team is always ready, aligned, and moving.

**Taglines:**
1. "Ready when it counts."
2. "Know where everyone stands. Know what to do."
3. "The workspace that rallies your team."

**Why this becomes a real SaaS brand:** Implies a daily ritual — you muster daily. Maps perfectly to the AI briefing layer. "Have you checked Muster today?" is a natural phrase. The ritual positioning creates habit and retention.

**Why better than ZeroOrigins OS:** Can be said by any founder, any age, in any industry. ZeroOrigins OS has a ceiling — it feels like software for software builders.

---

### #5 — SIGHTLINE `54/70`

**Homepage hero line:** A clear line of sight to everything that matters.

**1-line product description:** Sightline gives small teams a unified, AI-enhanced view of their work, revenue, and relationships — so no lead goes cold, no task goes untracked, and no priority goes unseen.

**Taglines:**
1. "No blind spots. No missed signals."
2. "See everything. Miss nothing."
3. "The clear view your team needed."

**Why this becomes a real SaaS brand:** Visual metaphor is enduring — doesn't depend on AI trends, doesn't feel like a buzzword, works equally well for a 5-person agency and a 50-person scale-up. Can own "business clarity" as a category without boxing itself in.

**Why better than ZeroOrigins OS:** Sightline positions the product for buyers, not builders. ZeroOrigins OS requires a pitch. Sightline explains itself.
